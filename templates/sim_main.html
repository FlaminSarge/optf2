Open Invitation

Welcome to the open test of the new OPTF2 inventory layer and profile search tool. Type in a name, ID, or other keyword for the user who's inventory you wish to see. Please report any problems or suggestions to the official OPTF2 group forum

Places of particular interest are the ones not already supported by OPTF2 in the main navigation such as Valve's games like TF2, DOTA 2, and Portal 2 but third party games such as Spiral Knights or SMNC, and your main Steam inventory. Supporting those seamlessly is the main objective of this project. Because of that, Op Inv viewing of Valve's games are temporarily disabled until the test is complete. Thank you for your participation. -- Lagg
